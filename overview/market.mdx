## Market Overview

The global AI and market research industries are undergoing a shift toward solutions that prioritize accessibility, cost efficiency, and scalability. While AI capabilities are advancing rapidly, most deployments remain concentrated in app- or web-based products, limiting reach among non-technical and low-infrastructure user groups.

<Card>
  <p>
    Voice-first AI represents a natural evolution, aligning advanced intelligence with the most
    widely adopted communication channel in the world.
  </p>
</Card>

## Market Drivers

- Increasing demand for AI in daily, non-technical workflows  
- Declining effectiveness and rising costs of human-led operations  
- Widespread global penetration of mobile and landline phones

## Market Opportunity

By lowering adoption friction and removing dependency on apps or digital literacy, voice-based AI can unlock new user bases at scale. As conversational models improve in depth and adaptability, this market is positioned for sustained growth.

  The largest AI opportunity lies not in smarter models alone, but in broader access.
